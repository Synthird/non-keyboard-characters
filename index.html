Arrows

  • Down: ↓
  • Greater than or equal to: ≥
  • Left: ←
  • Left and right: ↔
  • Less than or equal to: ≤
  • Redo or arrow Right circle: ↻
  • Right: →
  • Undo, Refresh, or arrow left circle: ↺
  • Up: ↑
  • Up and down: ↕

Poker cards

  • Clubs: ♣
  • Diamonds: ♦
  • Heart: ♥
  • Spade: ♠

Currency

  • Cent: ¢
  • Euro: €
  • Pounds: £
  • Yen: ¥

Math and science

  • Approximately: ≈
  • Because/since: ∵
  • Belongs to: ∈
  • Degrees: °
  • Delta: Δ
  • Division: ÷
  • Empty set: ∅
  • Gamma: γ
  • Infinity: ∞
  • Intersection of two: ⋂
  • Integral: ∫
  • Minus-plus: ∓
  • Mu: μ
  • Nabla: ∇
  • Ohms: Ω
  • Plus-minus: ±
  • Pi: π
  • Product: ∏
  • Square root or radical: √
  • Sum of: ∑
  • Therefore: ∴
  • Theta: θ
  • Union: ⋃
  • Not equal to: ≠

Legal

  • Copyright: ©
  • Trademark: ™
  • Registered trademark: ®

Music

  • Eighth note: ♪
  • Flat: ♭
  • Natural: ♮
  • Sharp: ♯

Other

  • Bullet/dot: •
  • Checkmark: ✓
  • Equilibrium: ⇌
  • Non-filled dot: ∘
  • Star (Filled): ★
  • Star (Not filled): ☆
  • Up Equilibrium: ⥮
