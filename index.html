Arrows

  • Down: ↓
  • Greater than or equal to: ≥
  • Left: ←
  • Left and right: ↔
  • Less than or equal to: ≤
  • Redo or arrow Right circle: ↻
  • Right: →
  • Undo, Refresh, or arrow left circle: ↺
  • Up: ↑
  • Up and down: ↕
